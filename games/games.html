About Me

I, Sangeeta Lall, am currently pursuing game design and screenwriting at Hunter College.

From a young age I always knew that I wanted to pursue a career in the arts. I have a deep love of storytelling spanning from those told in an interactive space or on the big screen. I aspire to tell my own stories through video games and films.

This website serves as a love letter to the media that has shaped me as a creator.

Why "Astro Daisy" ?

If there is one thing I truly admire, it is the Universe around us. As a kid, I was always fascinated by stars and celestial objects and the way in which they came to be. There is so much more out there than our individual selves. It puts things into perspective.

Daisies are the flower related to my birth month. The daisy symbolizes the start of a new beginning, I hope to continue to grow as an artist.

This website is still a work in progress.
